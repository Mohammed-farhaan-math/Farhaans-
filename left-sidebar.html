Funding

How can u assist our mission

At OmerCo we hate the idea of commercializing or gatekeeping research. Research is too exclusive even in countries where research is fairly popular. OmerCo wants to change that. We dont want Research to be a unknown path that those interested can only go down if they know smone in academia personally. This is not an effective way to spread Research culture. We also want to provide a means of escape for those stuck in the constant hassle of absorbing textbooks and questions for the hopes of getting a good job in the next placements season. We actively look for alternative Paths that we encourage our member researchers to follow. To keep all this free, we need money in one of two ways to afford subscriptions and more 1) Either we plaster the entire website with ads until its un-usable 2) Or we request funding from the public

Some other ways you can support is by subscribing to our Youtube channel and our Instagram Page. Youtube: https://youtube.com/@mohammedfarhaan9410?si=20ygjjqFx_nDs17s
