Farhaan's Fiscal Fuckery

FFF is a Program initiated to handle all Projects at the intersection of Pure Math and Finance . We Focus on Mathematical Modelling and Applications of Algebraic Topology.

Study of the Inverse Galois Problem

This Class is dedicated to the study of modern Group Theory's most important open problem. We Focus on the ingenious methods used to relate IGP to very different fields of Math

  • Find out more

Study of Minimal Surfaces

This Class is dedicated to the study of Minimal Surfaces and Their Geometry. We focus on how to interpret general problems about minimization as a minimal surface question

  • Find out more

Study of Co-Logic

Co-Logic is used to reason about infinite structures and their properties. We Study CoLogic to find a way to reason about Cardinalities without contradiction
